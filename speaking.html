What I like to talk about

  • Designing engaging learning experiences for technical audiences
  • Gamification, scavenger hunts, and other interactive formats
  • Using AI as a creative partner in learning design
  • Designing onboarding and learning for connection, not just compliance
  • Accessibility, inclusion, and practical learner support

Ways to use these materials

If you attended one of my sessions, this page is a place to revisit the main ideas and access the public-release resources. If you are looking for a speaker, it gives a quick sense of the topics I cover and the kind of learning experiences I create.

Much of the content on this site is meant to support conference attendees, so the resources are kept concise, reusable, and easy to navigate.
