My approach

I believe learning works best when people are curious, engaged, and able to connect new ideas to meaningful experiences. Whether I am designing onboarding programs, conference presentations, gamified learning experiences, or AI-enabled learning tools, my goal is the same: help people navigate unfamiliar territory with confidence.

Much of my professional work is proprietary, so the examples here focus on the strongest public-release material: conference sessions, infographics, passports, handouts, and presentation decks that already tell the story well.

What I bring

  • Experience designing learning for technical and professional audiences
  • A focus on connection, collaboration, and learner engagement
  • Practical experience with gamification, onboarding, and emerging technologies
  • Strong grounding in adult learning principles and learning science
  • Commitment to accessibility, inclusion, and real-world application
