About this archive

This site is designed to be polished, readable, and accessible. It avoids flashy clutter in favor of clear content hierarchy, keyboard-friendly navigation, and responsive layouts that hold up on desktop, tablet, and phone.

For now, the site focuses on the strongest public-release material: conference sessions, infographics, passports, handouts, and presentation decks that already carry your story in public form.

What visitors should learn fast

  • You design interactive learning experiences, not just slides.
  • You speak on onboarding, AI, and gamified learning.
  • You make complex systems feel navigable and human.
  • You bring a spark of geeky wonder without losing professionalism.
