APL Amazing Race

A large-scale scavenger-hunt style experience that used passports, progress trackers, hints, and collaborative location challenges to drive connection.

What the booklet supported

Participants used a passport booklet, progress tracker, and hint tracker to move across locations and complete challenges. The design made the experience feel playful while still being structured enough to manage a large group.

The overall event blended discovery, teamwork, and organizational connection into a single shared experience.

Highlights from the event

  • Passport and route map
  • Progress and hint tracking
  • Team-based puzzle stations
  • A finale that rewarded speed and collaboration
