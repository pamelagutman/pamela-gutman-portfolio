Virtual onboarding research

A public-facing summary of the learner experience research that informed later hybrid onboarding design.

What the study explored

The project surveyed how people experienced onboarding while working remotely and in hybrid environments, then looked for themes that could inform future onboarding design.

Its findings helped reinforce the importance of connection, spacing, and learner support during the transition into a new role.

Key themes

  • Connection matters.
  • People need clear structure.
  • Spacing helps reduce overwhelm.
  • Onboarding should balance information with belonging.
