Puzzles, Portals, and Prototypes

A practical session on using AI to brainstorm, narrow, iterate, and prototype gamified learning experiences while keeping human judgment in the loop.

What the talk covered

The session explores a four-step process: brainstorm, narrow the focus, iterate and refine, and finalize and prototype. The goal is to speed up ideation without surrendering design judgment.

It also includes examples of ways AI can help with themes, puzzle design, branching scenarios, and “what if” testing for learning experiences.

Why it matters

  • It helps beat blank-page syndrome.
  • It makes rapid prototyping easier.
  • It creates room for more iteration.
  • It still leaves the human in charge.
