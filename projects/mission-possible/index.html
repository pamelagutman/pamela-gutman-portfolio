Mission Possible

Energizing onboarding with an Amazing Race twist. The public materials show how the session used a mission passport, live challenges, and a framework others could borrow for their own programs.

Session structure

The deck opens with a briefing, moves into the live challenge, then ends with debrief, idea exchange, and takeaways. The passport included a map, progress tracker, hint tracker, and scratch paper so teams could move through the race in an organized way.

Reusable framework

  • Define the goal
  • Determine the content
  • Design the challenges
  • Handle logistics and testing
