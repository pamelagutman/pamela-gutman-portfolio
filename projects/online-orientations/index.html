Transforming Online Orientations

A public-release version of the Learning Solutions 2023 story, focused on brain-friendly pacing, collaboration, and hybrid-friendly connection-building.

What changed

The program moved from an in-person model to an online and then hybrid-friendly experience without losing the human touch. The presentation emphasizes spacing, connection, and interactivity rather than passive content dumps.

It also surfaced practical tools and patterns such as pre-recorded tours with live Q&A, breakout discussions, Kahoot!, and a scavenger hunt structure that linked new hires with organizational resources.

What the session demonstrated

  • Brain-friendly pacing and spacing
  • Interactive schedule and follow-on resources
  • Virtual tours, demos, and collaborative challenges
  • Hybrid inclusion and belonging as design goals
