Learning design with a map in one hand and a puzzle box in the other.

Pamela Gutman is a Learning Experience Designer, Speaker, and Builder of Interactive Adventures. She creates onboarding, training, and conference experiences that help people explore unfamiliar territory, connect with each other, and discover how to get things done.

Professional lens

Your work blends learning strategy, facilitation, interactive media, and practical implementation. The portfolio is designed to surface that blend clearly through case studies and session pages rather than burying it in a long list of deliverables.

  • Instructional design and learning strategy
  • Gamified and collaborative experiences
  • Conference speaking and resource sharing
  • Accessible, structured, mobile-friendly information design

Personal flavor

Outside of work, the archive can quietly hint at the geeky side of the map: science fiction, fantasy, escape rooms, tabletop roleplaying games, puzzles, and story-driven experiences. The goal is not cosplay. The goal is to let the site feel unmistakably human.
